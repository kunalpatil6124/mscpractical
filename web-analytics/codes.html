1) Mining Facebook: Analyzing Fan Pages, Examining Friendships: and More Overview, Exploring Facebook’s Social Graph API, Understanding the Social Graph API, Understanding the Open Graph Protocol, Analyzing Social Graph Connections, Analysing Facebook Pages, and Examining Friendships.

Step 1 : Go to https://developers.facebook.com

Step 2 : Click on developers tools

Step 3 : Click on the Graph API Explorer and login using facebook acoount details

Step 4 : After login click on Register/Get Started, Fill up the information, Choose Student and register successfully.

Step 5 : Select All Apps, and click on create app.

Step 6 : Enter all necessary details, APP name- select use case as Authenticate and request data from facebook and click on next.

Step 7 : Review app data and click on Create app.

Step 8 : Click on - Customize and authenticate on app dashboard.

Step 9 : Add features such as - email, date-of-birth,gender , etc.

Step 10 : Go to tools and click on -Graph API Explorer.

Step 11 : Click on generate Access token and then click on submit.

Step 12 : Click on permissions and add the permissions and then click on generate access token.

Step 13 : Add fields of the selected permissions and click on submit.

Step 14 : Click on Get Code and Copy android app code.
3) Segment Traffic: Which Social Channel Is Sending The Most Engaged New Users, Which Page Of Your Website Have Been Shared Most, Which URL Has The Best Engagement Matrix.

Step 1 : Go to chrome and Login to your google account.

Step 2 : Go to https://sites.google.com and click on create Blank Site.

Step 3 : Add Title For Example (Tourist Spots) And Click On Publish.

Step 4 : Add the web address ex.(tourist-spot123) and click on publish.

Step 5 : After Publishing click on view published site, and visit the site.

Step 6 : Go To The Google Analytics Tools

Step 7 : Click On Get Started

Step 8 : Create An Account For Ex(Travelling Tourism) The Click Next Button.

Step 9 : Add property details, ex.travelling tourism. then click on next.

Step 10 : Add bussiness details and click on next.

Step 11 : Click on other bussiness objective and then click I accept and submit.

Step 12 : Choose the platform as - Web, and add published site URL, stream name and click on create and continue.

Step 13 : Copy the google tag and paste it to the website footer using embed and publish it.

Step 14 : After publishing come to analytics page and click on test installation.

Step 15 : Click on next- continue to home, and check the analytics.
